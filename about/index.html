About Ashley

Ashley Carranza is an author and educator whose work is rooted in a simple belief: every child deserves stories that make them feel seen, capable, and inspired. Her books blend creativity with real classroom experience, giving young readers the tools to build confidence and fall in love with learning.

Before becoming a published author, Ashley spent years in the classroom watching firsthand how the right story at the right moment could change a student's entire outlook. That experience shapes everything she writes — from picture books that celebrate curiosity to coloring books designed to make learning feel like play.

Today, Ashley splits her time between writing, speaking at education conferences, and visiting schools where she works directly with students and teachers. She is passionate about literacy, creativity, and making sure every young reader knows their story matters.
